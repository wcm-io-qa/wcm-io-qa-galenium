       <h1 class="title title-section" data-emptytext="Headline">About adaptTo() 2013</h1> 
        
       <cq data-config="{&quot;path&quot;:&quot;/content/wcm-io-samples/en/conference/jcr:content/content/contentheadline&quot;,&quot;slingPath&quot;:&quot;/content/wcm-io-samples/en/conference/jcr:content/content/contentheadline.html&quot;,&quot;dialog&quot;:&quot;/apps/wcm-io-samples/core/components/content/common/contentHeadline/cq:dialog&quot;,&quot;dialogLoadingMode&quot;:&quot;auto&quot;,&quot;dialogLayout&quot;:&quot;auto&quot;,&quot;dialogSrc&quot;:&quot;/mnt/override/apps/wcm-io-samples/core/components/content/common/contentHeadline/_cq_dialog.html/content/wcm-io-samples/en/conference/jcr:content/content/contentheadline&quot;,&quot;type&quot;:&quot;wcm-io-samples/core/components/content/common/contentHeadline&quot;,&quot;isResponsiveGrid&quot;:false,&quot;csp&quot;:&quot;content|page/content|parsys/contentHeadline&quot;,&quot;editConfig&quot;:{&quot;listeners&quot;:{&quot;afteredit&quot;:&quot;sampleApp.authoring.listener.refreshAjaxContentAfterEdit&quot;}},&quot;ipeConfig&quot;:{}}" data-path="/content/wcm-io-samples/en/conference/jcr:content/content/contentheadline"></cq> 
      </div> 
      <div class="contentRichText section"> 
       <p>adaptTo() is a technical meetup focused on the technical stack of <a href="http://sling.apache.org/" target="_blank">Apache Sling</a> including <a href="http://jackrabbit.apache.org/" target="_blank">Apache Jackrabbit</a> and <a href="http://felix.apache.org/" target="_blank">Apache Felix</a> and is addressed to all developers using this stack or parts of it.</p> 
       <p>2013 we'll have the third edition of adaptTo() and will again be located in Berlin, Germany. adaptTo() was successful in 2012 and will take place at a new location this year. The popular Kulturbrauerei offers more space to improve your experience. The location and the support of our main sponsor Adobe will again give you a platform to get in touch with some of the most experienced Sling, Jackrabbit and Felix developers and share your own experience with the community.</p> 
       <p>Additional sessions will cover commercial implementations like <a href="http://www.adobe.com/products/cq.html" target="_blank">Adobe CQ</a> and CRX, with particular focus on how these products leverage the architecture.</p> 
       <p><strong>Date</strong></p> 
       <p>23.-25.September 2013</p> 
       <p><strong>Location</strong></p> 
       <p>Kulturbrauerei Berlin<br /> More info on location</p> 
       <p><strong>Ticketprice</strong></p> 
       <p>149,95&euro;</p> 
       <p><strong>Next steps</strong></p> 
       <p>Call for papers is ending 6. May 2013<br /> Submit your paper here</p> 
       <p><strong>Registration</strong></p> 
       <p>Register for tickets here</p> 
       <p><strong>Sponsors</strong></p> 
       <p>This event is sponsored by <a href="http://www.adobe.com" target="_blank">Adobe Systems</a> and <a href="http://www.pro-vision.de/" target="_blank">pro!vision GmbH</a> which also acts as host.</p>
